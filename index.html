The first known NFT community takeover!

This may be the most unique story in the NFT world! The story of the first known community takeover! Many members originally joined the community based on the chill environment and because the original roadmap promised utility at some point.

The week of launch there were indeed several factors that were out of everyone's control; such as exorbitant gas fees and a down market for NFTs. For a minute it looked promising though, minting just around 1k in the first 24 horus. But the tide quickly turned. Staking was removed from the roadmap plans. The devs at the time became stale. In desperation, they started to throw out ETH on poorly planned Twitter promos.

The bear holders were itching to have anything exiting to show new members; but any suggestions provided by mods or the community were falling on deaf ears. Doubt was cast on whether or not there was any intention on actually maintaining the project going forward, or if they just lacked all the necessary skills to pull it off. As a result, the core bear holders took matters into their own hands. Creating a united front, the community began to demand answers, direction or ANYTHING to guarantee that the project had a future. In time and after considerable pressure, the devs eventually waived the white flag, stepped down and relinquished the keys to the kingdom. The community took over Chill Bears Society and successfully go things back on track!
